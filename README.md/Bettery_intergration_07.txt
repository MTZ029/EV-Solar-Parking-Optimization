Simple BESS Price Arbitrage

A rule-based battery controller was implemented.

The battery charges when the electricity price is below a predefined threshold and discharges when the price exceeds a predefined threshold.

The SOC trajectory shows the expected behavior:

- Charging during low-price periods.
- Discharging during high-price periods.
- Respecting minimum and maximum SOC limits.

The battery reaches both SOC boundaries several times during the day, confirming that the control logic is functioning correctly.

However, the controller only uses electricity prices and ignores:

- Building demand
- PV production
- EV charging demand

Therefore, this serves only as a baseline strategy before integrating PV and EV information.
