Notebook 10 – Flexible EV charging LP

A linear programming model was formulated for smart EV charging.

Decision variables:
- Charging power of each EV during each hour.

Objective:
- Minimize total charging cost using hourly electricity prices.

Constraints:
- EV charging only allowed while connected.
- Maximum charger power limit.
- Required charging energy must be delivered before departure.

Results:
The optimizer successfully shifted charging away from arrival times and concentrated charging during the cheapest
hours of the day.

Compared to ASAP charging, the charging profile changed significantly while delivering the same total energy
demand.

This demonstrates the value of EV flexibility and provides the basis for integration with PV generation and
    battery storage.
